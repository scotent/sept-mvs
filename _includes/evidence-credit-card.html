Corporate or personal credit card statements can be submitted.

For a credit card statement to be fully compliant as evidence of payment, it must:

  • Be a formal statement (either a paper copy or online print-out), not simply a transaction listing
  • Clearly show the issuer's name, address and logo
  • Clearly show the account holder's name and details
  • Clearly show relevant transaction dates, amounts and payees that correspond to other evidence, such as bookings, invoices or receipts.

Please note: You must also submit a corresponding bank statement showing payment of the relevant credit card transactions, or wage slip/payroll information confirming payment of the transactions as expenses (along with a corresponding Bacs transaction list, if necessary).
