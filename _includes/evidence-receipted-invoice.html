For a bank statement to be fully compliant as evidence of payment, it must:

  • Be stamped 'paid' or 'received with thanks', with the supplier's company stamp.
  • Be signed by an authorised accountable officer, such as a company secretary, director, partner or authorised signatory.
  • Include the invoice number, payment value, payment date and method of payment.
