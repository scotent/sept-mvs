Scottish Development International is committed to making its services accessible to everyone, including those with special needs.

If you use a screen reader, or some other device to help you view the Internet, you might prefer to use the text-only version of this site. It is just as complete and up-to-date as the graphical version.

Every web page on this site should conform to Level-A of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. Additionally, we are working towards Level-AA for the more popular sections of the web site. Our ultimate goal is to achieve Level-AAA throughout the entire site.

However, we're not perfect, so if you spot a problem please let us know by emailing us (investment@scotent.co.uk) or completing the feedback form. Alternatively, please call Scottish Development International on 0800 917 9534 form within the UK or +44 141 228 2828 if you are overseas. We'll fix the problem as soon as we can.
