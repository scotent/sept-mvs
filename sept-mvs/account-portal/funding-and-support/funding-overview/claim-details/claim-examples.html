This information details the minimum evidence required to support the eligible project costs identified in the contract approved for your project.

You can email your evidence, along with your completed claim for payment form, to contractclaims@scotent.co.uk. If you have any questions about your claim, please contact the contract specialist named in your contract.

Supplier/consultant costs

Invoices from a supplier or consultant must clearly show:

  • The total cost of the product or service
  • Any VAT element of the cost separately (VAT costs are ineligible and cannot be included in the claim)

All invoices from suppliers or consultants must be accompanied by evidence of payment that clearly shows the invoice amount and the name of the supplier/consultant.

Salaries

For projects part-funded by the European Structural and Investment Funds (ESIF) Programme 2014-2020, you can only claim the salaries of staff who work 100% of their time on approved projects. Paragraph 3.3 of your contract will confirm if your project is ESIF funded or not.
Please note: you can only claim for gross/basic pay; other payments, such as commission or bonuses, cannot be included.

ESIF-funded projects

For each individual salary you claim, you must submit:

  • An employee job description, plus a signed letter from an authorised accountable officer confirming that 100% of this employee's time was spent working on the project.
  • Individual wage slips, or a company payroll listing, which must clearly show the cost breakdown of each individual salary being claimed.

Non ESIF-funded projects

For each individual salary you claim, you must submit

  • Individual wage slips, or a company payroll listing, which must clearly show the cost breakdown of each individual salary being claimed.

For staff spending only part of their time working on approved projects, you must also submit:

  • Detailed time sheets (or information from a time recording system) specifying the amount of time spent on the project, which must be signed by the employee and countersigned by their manager/director.

All salary claims must be accompanied by evidence of payment that clearly shows the salary amount and the individual payee.

Please note: If the individual salary payment is part of a bulk Bacs payment (for example, as 'Payroll'), then you must submit both the complete Bacs transaction list and a bank statement. The overall total sum on the transaction list must match the Bacs payment as it appears on your bank statement
