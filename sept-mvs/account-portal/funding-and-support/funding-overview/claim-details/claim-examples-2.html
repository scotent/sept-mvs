We require evidence of your costs (invoices/payslips etc.) and matched evidence of your payment of these costs (Bank and/or bacs statements/Credit card statements etc.)

This information details the minimum evidence required to support the eligible project costs identified in the contract approved for your project.

If you have any questions about your claim, please contact the contract specialist named in your contract.
