Ready to start your claim

To complete your claim, you'll need:

  • Payslips for each employee whose salary you are claiming for.
  • A Bacs or payroll statement that shows the salary payments for these employees, if appropriate.
  • A bank statement that shows the Bacs statement total, or the salary payments.
  • A completed reconciliation spreadsheet for this claim. You can download a reconciliation spreadsheet if you don't have one
  • A signed copy of the claim form for this claim. You can download a blank claim form if you don't have one.
Only someone who is authorised to act on behalf of the business can submit a claim. If you aren't authorised to do so, you can send the completed claim to someone who is, one all files have been added.

You can save the claim at any point and return to complete it later.
