Your account services all in one place

Your account services all in one place In My account, you can view and manage key elements of your business and account. Whether you’re at home, in the office or café, you can access:

  • Services – for example, your funding claims and support
  • Business details – such as key contacts and business information
  • Account settings and preferences

Your account is a secure place, tailored to provide access to the Scottish Enterprise support services for your business needs. If you’re awarded grants or funding, you can manage the process, make claims and upload supporting evidence quickly and easily.
